Program Description

White Rabbit Ventures LLC operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with real estate clients and prospects regarding property inquiries, appointment scheduling, and related real estate services.

Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number and consenting to receive text messages, you agree to receive SMS messages from White Rabbit Ventures LLC. Message frequency varies based on your inquiries and our follow-up schedule.

Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us directly at the information below.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. White Rabbit Ventures LLC is not responsible for any charges incurred from your wireless carrier.

Data Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All opt-in data and consent information will be kept confidential and used solely to operate this messaging program.
